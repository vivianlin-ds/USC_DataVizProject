About Page

We choose to look at modeling inflation and tourism because our group is all Applied Data Science majors, and we have learned how to use models to develop insights into data. Because visualizations are a powerful tool to communicate findings from our models, we felt it would be interesting to see how our models perform, and this has been useful practice to work with large and impactful datasets. We also all enjoy traveling!

Data used

1. The World Bank - A Global Dataset of Inflation: Inflation
2. The World Bank - International Tourism: Tourism
3. World Map GeoJSON: GeoJSON
