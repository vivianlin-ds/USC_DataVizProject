For our project, we are working on creating an interactive dashboard for users to view and analyze inflation and tourism datasets for various countries. Our primary objective with this dashboard was to model the potential relationship between inflation and the tourist arrivals in a country. Consequently, we were successfully able to predict the inflation rate, tourist arrivals, and tourist departures for the next four years. Additionally, we created pages in our dashboard to break down inflation and tourism data specifically, in order for users to visualize the large amount of data that we ingested for this project. Our chosen datasets were a great opportunity to explore using projections for world maps since our inflation rates are all by country. We wanted to highlight the use of interactive and dynamic code that we have learned about in class, because it is commonly used in the dashboards we looked at during our research.

Page 1 - Inflation

  1. Choropleth Map on Headline Consumer Inflation Rates Plots each country's headline consumer inflation rates (HCPI) on a choropleth map. Hovering over each country will show the specific percentages of the country and clicking on the country will show detailed information on inflation in up to four time series line charts.
  2. Time Series Line Chart on other Inflation Indicators (up to four for each country) Shows up to 4 inflation indicators (Official Core consumer price index, Food price index, Energy price index, and/or Producer price index) for the specific country clicked by the user. Each chart is accompanied by a brief explanation of the index. The charts will update automatically based on the different countries the user clicked on.

Page 2 - Tourism

All charts respond to the chosen year in the dropdown.
  1. Choropleth Map on Departure over Arrival Ratios Display the ratio of number of departures over number of arrivals on a choropleth map. This shows which countries have the unbalanced number of arriving/departing travelers.
  2. Pie Charts on Number of Arrivals/Departures in Each Continent Group the countries by continent and display the percentages of arrivals/departures by each continent. This shows which continent experiences the most arriving/departing travelers.
  3. Bar Charts on Top 20 Countries with Highest Arrivals/Departures Visualize the top 20 countries with the highest arrivals/departures and allow users to interact with the chart by filtering and sorting the 20 countries. Users can filter countries based on continent and if the continent does not have a country in the chart, the button is automatically disabled and darkened. Users can also sort the bars in descending or alphabetical order.

Page 3 - Inflation and Tourism Forecasting and Correlation

All charts respond to the chosen year in the dropdown.
  1. Bar Chart on Forecasting Inflation Index, Number of Arrivals/Departures, and Expenditures in USD. SARIMAX based forecasting for the next 4 years
  2. Table showing the values of the bar chart with predicted values highlighted The forecasted values, averaged for each year
  3. Heatmap showing correlation matrix between inflation index and indicators for tourism Analysis of the correlation between the Inflation and other Tourism indicators.
